About

This app uses specialized AI agents to generate educational content. Each agent has a specific role: Course Structure Designer, Lecture Notes Writer, Tutorial Exercise Designer, Practical Exercise Designer, and Quiz Creator.

You can choose between OpenAI (GPT-4) or Google Gemini as your AI provider. Both providers offer high-quality content generation capabilities.
